# Privacy Policy for MarkdownResume

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025

MarkdownResume is designed to prioritize your privacy. We believe your career history is personal data that should remain under your control.

**1. Data Collection**
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage statistics, or document contents to external servers. The application functions entirely offline.

**2. Local Storage**
All files, resumes, and assets created or managed by MarkdownResume are stored locally on your Mac in the file paths you select. We do not have access to these files.

**3. Third-Party Services**
MarkdownResume does not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
